Delete Your Account - Chibi Colosseum

Last Updated: March 2026

If you would like to request deletion of your account and associated data for Chibi Colosseum, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to:

seasluggames@gmail.com

Include the following information:

  • Your Player ID (if available)
  • Your character name
  • A short message requesting account deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Player progress (level, stats, inventory)
  • Cloud save data
  • Linked account identifiers (Unity Authentication)

What Data May Be Retained

  • Anonymous technical logs (for debugging and security)
  • Data required to comply with legal obligations

Retention Period

Deletion requests are processed within 7 days. Some minimal data may be retained for up to 30 days for backup and recovery purposes.

Guest Accounts

Guest accounts are stored locally on your device. You can delete this data by uninstalling the game or clearing app storage.

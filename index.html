Chibi Colosseum (“the Game”, “we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the game.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: anonymous player ID and linked account identifiers if you choose to sign in or link an account later.
  • Gameplay Data: character data, progress, inventory, settings, and other game-related information required for normal play.
  • Device and Technical Data: device type, operating system, crash logs, and diagnostics if enabled by the services used by the game.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and load your game progress.
  • To provide gameplay features and account functionality.
  • To improve game stability, performance, and player experience.
  • To support account linking, authentication, and recovery features where available.

3. Third-Party Services

Chibi Colosseum may use third-party services, including Unity services, to support game functionality.

  • Unity Authentication
  • Unity Player Accounts if enabled
  • Other Unity services used by the game as development continues

These services may collect technical and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Unity Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Data Storage

  • Game data may be stored locally on your device.
  • Some data may be stored or processed using Unity services or other online services used by the game.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

5. Your Choices

  • You may choose to play as a guest where supported.
  • You may choose to sign in or link an account later if such features are available.
  • You may stop using the game at any time.
  • You may contact us regarding questions about your data.

6. Children’s Privacy

Chibi Colosseum is not intended for children under 13, or the minimum digital consent age in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children in violation of applicable law.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last Updated” date on this page.
